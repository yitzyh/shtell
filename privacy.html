Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 19, 2024

Introduction

Shtell ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in with Apple, we collect:

  • Apple ID identifier (anonymized)
  • Email address (optional, based on your Apple ID settings)
  • Display name (if provided)

User-Generated Content

We store content you create within the app:

  • Saved webpages and bookmarks
  • Comments and replies
  • Quoted text from webpages
  • User profile information
  • Follow relationships with other users

Usage Data

We may collect:

  • Pages you browse and interact with
  • App usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Device information (iOS version, device model)
  • Crash reports and error logs

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Shtell service
  • Enable social features (comments, saves, follows)
  • Personalize your browsing experience
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Prevent abuse and enforce our Terms of Service

Data Storage

Your data is currently stored using Apple CloudKit services. We may migrate to Amazon Web Services (AWS) in future versions. You will be notified of any data storage changes through app updates.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Your data may be shared:

  • Publicly: Comments and profile information you choose to make public
  • With other users: When you interact with their content (likes, saves, follows)
  • Service providers: Apple (CloudKit), AWS (future), for infrastructure and hosting
  • Legal requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your saved content
  • Opt out of data collection (by not using the app)

Data Retention

We retain your data while your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

Children's Privacy

Shtell is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Apple Sign In with secure authentication
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app and on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@shtell.app

California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what personal information we collect and the right to deletion. Contact us to exercise these rights.
