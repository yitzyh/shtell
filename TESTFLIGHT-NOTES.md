# TestFlight Release Notes

## Version 1.1.7

```
NEW BROWSEFORWARD FEATURE:
- Select Categories: Long-press the orange arrow to choose topics for your discovery feed
- BrowseForward Mode: Tap orange up arrow to explore articles from your selected categories
- Pull Down Gesture: Swipe down to move forward through history

SIGN IN:
Try to save or comment on any webpage to sign in with Apple

COMMENTS & SOCIAL:
- Quote: Highlight text on any page to quote it in your comment
- Comment: View and post comments on any webpage
- Reply: Respond to other users' comments

BROWSER:
- Save: Bookmark any webpage with one tap
- Share to Shtell: Share any webpage from Safari and other apps to load in browser

---

BUG REPORTS:
1. Take a screenshot while using the app
2. When prompted to save, tap "Share Beta Feedback"
3. Add your description and submit with screenshot attached

You can also leave feedback anytime from the TestFlight app.
```
